Products

Records

The Turning Point has one full floor of thousands of categorized and alphabetical organized LPs, ranging from “very good” to “near mint” condition. We carry albums from the 1950s to the present, with special emphasis on records from the 1960s and 1970s in all genres. The average price for these records is $ 5.00 – $ 10.00. On the store’s main floor we have several bins of “New Arrivals” records that are available for the first time. We have an inflow of hundreds of new LPs every week. We also have a Collector’s section offering rare, out-of-print, and Import LPs.

Thousands of LPs - all in great shape and all at affordable prices.

CDs

The Turning Point Ottawa music store offers thousands of CDs in Classic Rock, Alternative Rock, Soul, Jazz, Blues, Folk, Country, Alt-Country, Metal, World, Celtic, Reggae, Male & Female Vocalists, Classical and Soundtracks. Our prices range from $ 4.00 – $ 10.00. All CDs are guaranteed. We specialize in hard to find CDs, Imports, and Box Sets not carried in conventional CD stores.

All our CDs are repackged in new cases.

DVDs

We have a huge selection of quality movies in both formats. Prices run $ 3.00 – $ 7.95 for most films. Categories include Dramas, Action, Horror, Sci-fi, Westerns, Comedies, Musicals, Concert films, Children’s films, Foreign Films, and Documentaries. We also sell popular TV series, with particular interest in older classics (from Get Smart to the Sopranos), current favourites, and British BBC series.
